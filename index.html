what is skalo?

Skalo is a program that provides teachers with an easy-to-use digital tool to individualized education plans for children with disability so all children can access, participate and thrive in early education.

CUSTOMIZE accommodations according to each students´ needs, interests, abilities and opportunities of improvement.

MONITORS implementation of accommodations in the classroom and at home

motivates teachers and institutions to implement best- inclusive practices

CONSOLIDATED information on disabilities in early education and best practices

INTEGRATES families into the design, implementation and monitoring of individualized educational plans connecting families with teachers and institutions.

why skalo?

Skalo is a practical tool that teachers of children with disabilities in early education needed to provide high quality inclusive education that promotes access, participation and progress for all children in the classroom.

download skalo

Skalo suggests the most appropriate reasonable accommodations for each student in the classroom

The family creates their child´s profile based not only on their child´s disability, but also on his/her abilities, areas of improvement, interest and personal characteristics.

Teachers perform the initial pedagogical assessment to identify key areas of improvement.

With this information, Skalo suggests best reasonable accommodations that allow children to participate in all curricular activities and reach their full potential.

Skalo helps monitor implementation and effectiveness of accommodations so plan can be adjusted if needed.

Skalo offers:

Customized Accommodations

An individualized educational plan with accommodations suggested for EACH child

Monitoring

An easy-to-use tool that facilitates continuous monitoring of the efficacy of accommodations

reports

Creation of reports based on monitoring and observations, saving time and allowing for more accuracy.

Home Tips

Recommendations for families to help students become more independent at home.

Inclusion Tips

Inclusion best practices to foster and effective and high-quality inclusive education.

Library

Access to key resources about disabilities, difficulties observed in the classroom, reasonable accommodations and best practices.

Family Integration

Facilitates the interaction of families, teachers and institutions in the planning, implementation and monitoring of accommodations.

Skalo impacts all the education stakeholders:

Skalo has a positive impact at multiple levels: Promotes an effective and high-quality inclusive education right from the start.

Motivates teachers and provides them with an easy-to-use tool that allow all children to access, participate and thrive.

Contributes to families´ well-being allowing the to actively participate in their children education process.

Children that can thrive in their education can actively participate in society.
